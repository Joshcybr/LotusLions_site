About Us

Founded in 2017, the Lotus Lions have quickly grown into one of Cape Town’s most promising junior volleyball teams. With a passion for the sport and a drive to succeed, our team has competed in numerous tournaments, bringing home multiple trophies and proudly representing at the prestigious Club Champs.

In 2025, Lotus Lions entered an exciting new era under fresh ownership — bringing renewed energy, vision, and leadership to the team. As a young and dynamic squad, we're fueled by potential, unity, and the hunger to grow into champions of the future.

With a focus on development, discipline, and teamwork, the Lotus Lions are more than just a team — we're a family. Join us as we continue to rise and inspire the next generation of volleyball stars.

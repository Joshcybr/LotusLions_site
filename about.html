Who Are We?

The Lotus Lions were born in 2017 with a vision: to build a youth volleyball team that blends raw talent with unbreakable spirit. From local courts to national arenas, we’ve carved out a name as one of Cape Town’s fiercest junior squads — one serve, one block, and one point at a time.

Our journey took a bold leap in 2025 when new leadership infused the team with fresh ambition and unstoppable drive. Now, every training session, every match, and every huddle is a step toward something greater — **becoming the champions we know we can be**.

But we’re more than just volleyball players. We are **brothers in arms**, bonded by sweat, shared victories, and a passion for the game. We lift each other up — and we rise together. 🏐💪

Want to be part of the pride?

We’re always scouting for talent, passion, and heart. Come train with us and see if you’ve got what it takes!
